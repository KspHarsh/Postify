SSC GD Constable 2025 Exam: Complete Guide for 53,690 Vacancies

Recruitment for General Duty Constable positions in BSF, CISF, CRPF, SSB, ITBP, AR, SSF, and NCB.

The Staff Selection Commission of India conducts the SSC GD exam annually to select candidates for the General Duty Constable positions in BSF, CISF, CRPF, SSB, ITBP, AR, SSF, and NCB. This year, the exam is being conducted for the recruitment of 53,690 constable posts. Of these, 48,320 vacancies are for male posts and 5,370 for female posts.

SSC GD Constable Eligibility Criteria

Educational Qualification

Candidates applying for GD Constable posts must have passed 10th class (Matriculation) or equivalent from a recognized board.

Age Limit (as on 01/01/2025)

Candidates must be between 18 to 23 years. They should not have been born earlier than 02-01-2002 and later than 01-01-2007. Age relaxation is applicable for reserved categories.

SSC GD Constable 2025 Selection Process

  • Stage 1: Written Examination (Computer Based)
  • Stage 2: Physical Efficiency Test (PET)
  • Stage 3: Physical Standard Test (PST)
  • Stage 4: Medical Test & Document Verification

SSC GD 2025 Exam Pattern (Computer-Based)

The online exam will have 80 questions for 160 marks, to be completed in 60 minutes. There is a negative marking of 0.25 marks for each wrong answer.
