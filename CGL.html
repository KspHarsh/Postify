SSC CGL 2025 Exam: Complete Guide for 17,727 (Tentative) Vacancies

Recruitment for Group 'B' and Group 'C' posts in various Ministries/Departments of the Government of India.

The Staff Selection Commission of India conducts the SSC Combined Graduate Level (CGL) exam annually to select candidates for various Group ‘B’ and Group ‘C’ posts in different Ministries/Departments/Organisations. This year, the exam is expected to be conducted for the recruitment of 17,727 posts (tentative).

SSC CGL Vacancy 2025 (Tentative)

A total of 17,727 tentative vacancies have been announced for various Group 'B' and 'C' posts. The final, post-wise vacancy distribution will be released by the commission on its official website in due course.

SSC CGL Eligibility Criteria

Educational Qualification

Candidates applying for SSC CGL posts must have a Bachelor's Degree from a recognized University or equivalent. There are specific qualifications for certain posts like Assistant Audit Officer (AAO) and Junior Statistical Officer (JSO).

Age Limit (as on 01/08/2025)

The age limit varies for different posts, generally ranging from 18 to 32 years. The specific age limits are detailed in the official notification. Age relaxation is applicable for reserved categories.

SSC CGL 2025 Selection Process

  • Stage 1: Tier-I Examination (Computer Based)
  • Stage 2: Tier-II Examination (Computer Based)
  • Stage 3: Document Verification
  • Stage 4: Medical Examination (As required for specific posts)

SSC CGL 2025 Tier-I Exam Pattern

The online exam will have 100 questions for 200 marks, to be completed in 60 minutes. There is a negative marking of 0.50 marks for each wrong answer.

SSC CGL 2025 Tier-II Exam Pattern

Tier-II will include conducting Paper-I, Paper-II & Paper-III in separate shifts/days. Paper-I is compulsory for all the posts. Paper-II will be only for those candidates who apply for the posts of Junior Statistical Officer (JSO). Paper-III will be only for those candidates who are shortlisted in Tier-I for Assistant Audit Officer/ Assistant Accounts Officer posts.

SSC CGL Tier-II Cutoff 2025 (Expected)

After the Tier-II exam, the Staff Selection Commission releases category-wise cutoffs to shortlist candidates for the final merit list and subsequent document verification. The merit list is prepared based on the overall performance in Section-I and Section-II of Paper-I. The Computer Knowledge Test and Data Entry Speed Test are qualifying in nature.
